### 2. Description

**Promotional Text** (max 170 characters - appears above description, editable without new version):
```
Track which tors you've bagged and add photos of your visit.  
```

**Full Description** (max 4,000 characters):
```
Select the categories of tors you'd like to visit. Use the photo scanning tool to find photos of tors you've already bagged. Keep track of your visits with a dedicated album. Use maps to get directions, with links to other maps and information about each tor. 

FEATURES

• Complete Tor Database
Thanks to the good people at Tors of Dartmoor, browse all tors with detailed information including height, OS grid reference, coordinates, access status, and links.

• Interactive Map
View all tors on an interactive map with colour-coded markers. Teal markers for publicly accessible tors, orange for private land, and green for tors you've visited. Includes satellite and road map views, compass tracking, and one-tap location centering.

• Search and List
Sort tors by height, alphabetically, distance from you, or directionally (north to south, east to west, etc.). Filter to hide visited tors and use integrated search to find specific tors quickly.

• Visit Tracking
Mark tors as visited with the date of your visit. Track your progress toward completing all tors of Dartmoor. Photos can be added to one of your albums. Makes it easy to share progress with fellow baggers.

• Mark tors you've already bagged with photoscan
Use the photos scanning tool to find photos taken within 100m of a tor and mark it as visited.

• iCloud Sync & Sharing
Sync your checklist across all your Apple devices with iCloud. 

• Navigation Integration
Open any tor directly in Apple Maps, OS maps, or Google Maps. Get turn-by-turn navigation.

• Built-in compass and line of sight
Use your phone's compass and a line on the map to help you identify tors in the distance. 

• Works Offline
All tor data is available offline, perfect for exploring Dartmoor's remote areas where signal is limited.

• Distance Tracking
See how far each tor is from your current location (when location services are enabled).

Whether you're planning your next moorland walk or tracking your tor-bagging progress, Dartmoor Tors is your essential companion for exploring this stunning landscape.
```
